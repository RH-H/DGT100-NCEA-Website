Welcome to the Castlepoint Tourist Website. Here you can find information on Castlepoint, a beachside town on the Wairarapa Coast of the Wellington Region of New Zealand.

Castlepoint has many different places to go and explore such as the Castlepoint lighthouse, castle rock, a huge mountain right on the beach, and even a cave located underneath the lighthouse.
